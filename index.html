Co-Directors Rebecca von Duering, MS, CCC-SLP & Maura Philippone, MA, CCC-SLP

Founded by Maura Philippone in February 2023, supported by co-Director Rebecca von Duering, Forward Focus on Voice is a Voice Journal Club that meets monthly to connect and discuss current findings in voice research. The goal of this group is to collaborate and make research findings more accessible and applicable to clinical care for any progressional who helps clients with their voice (including ENTs, SLPs, Vocologists, Students, etc)

Speech and Language Pathology

A highly trained inpatient and outpatient SLP since 2008, I have special certifications in the PESL Accent Modification, SOS Approach to Feeding, MBSImp protocol for Modified Barium Swallow Studies, LSVT LOUD, PhoRTE, Hanen: It Takes Two to Talk and More Than Words, and Gender Affirming Voice Therapy.
